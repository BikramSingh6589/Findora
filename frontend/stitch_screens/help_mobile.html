Top FAQs

What should I do if I find an item? expand_more

Head to the 'Report' tab, snap a photo, and list the location. You'll earn XP once the owner verifies the match! 🚀

How do rewards work? expand_more

By helping others, you earn XP and 'Karma' tokens. These can be redeemed at the Campus Cafe for discounts! ☕

Is my data private? expand_more

Absolutely. We only share item details. Your personal contact info is kept hidden until a secure hand-off is agreed upon. 🔒
